How Do I Choose a Database?

Choosing from among the hundreds of databases that your library purchases can be daunting. Here are a few quick tips:

  • Not sure where to start? Try
    • Academic Search Complete
      (covers many different topics, includes scholarly and news articles)
    • Web of Knowledge
      (focuses on scholarly articles)
    • JSTOR
      (scholarly articles from different fields, most recent 3-5 years not included)
  • Use the links on the previous page to explore the resources to find databases and articles at your home campus
    • Hint: Look for phrases like “Recommended Databases”, “Most Popular”, “Frequently Used Databases”, or “Databases by Subject” to guide you.
